Videoquiz Maker

This app is an innovative user-friendly tool to enhance the learning process of students through interactive videos. The application allows you to embed interactive elements on the video, especially enables you to embed questions which interrupt the video playback. Another striking feature is that you can create animations and transition effects on the elements and on the video at any time during playback, this allows maximize the visualization of the content.

Videquiz Maker is a customized version of Mozilla Popcorn Maker.
